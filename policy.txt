--------------------------------------------------
1. COVERAGE DETAILS
--------------------------------------------------

This policy provides reimbursement for the following:

1.1 Hospitalization Coverage:
- In-patient hospitalization expenses exceeding ₹5,000
- Room rent up to ₹5,000 per day
- ICU charges up to ₹10,000 per day
- Nursing and service charges

1.2 Medical Treatment Coverage:
- Doctor consultation fees
- Medicines and consumables
- Diagnostic tests (lab reports, imaging)

1.3 Chronic Disease Coverage:
Covered chronic illnesses include:
- Diabetes (Type 1 & Type 2)
- Hypertension
- Cardiac diseases
- Kidney-related illnesses

1.4 Pre and Post Hospitalization:
- Pre-hospitalization: 30 days
- Post-hospitalization: 60 days

--------------------------------------------------
2. ELIGIBILITY CONDITIONS
--------------------------------------------------

- Patient must be a registered policyholder
- Policy must be active at the time of treatment
- Treatment must be medically necessary
- Prescription must be issued by a certified doctor
- Supporting lab reports must be available

--------------------------------------------------
3. EXCLUSIONS
--------------------------------------------------

The policy DOES NOT cover:

- Cosmetic or aesthetic treatments
- Dental procedures (unless due to accident)
- Experimental or unproven treatments
- Self-inflicted injuries
- Substance abuse related treatments

Pre-existing Disease Clause:
- Diseases existing before policy start are covered ONLY after 12 months waiting period

--------------------------------------------------
4. FINANCIAL LIMITS
--------------------------------------------------

- Maximum Annual Claim Limit: ₹2,00,000
- Maximum per hospitalization: ₹1,00,000
- Room rent limit: ₹5,000 per day
- ICU limit: ₹10,000 per day

Sub-limits:
- Doctor consultation: ₹2,000 per visit
- Lab tests combined limit: ₹10,000
- Medicines: As per actuals (within total limit)

--------------------------------------------------
5. CLAIM DOCUMENT REQUIREMENTS
--------------------------------------------------

The following documents are mandatory:

- Doctor's prescription
- Hospital bills (itemized)
- Lab reports
- Discharge summary
- Identity proof of patient

If any document is missing, claim may be rejected.

--------------------------------------------------
6. CLAIM EVALUATION RULES
--------------------------------------------------

The claim will be evaluated based on:

- Authenticity of documents
- Consistency between diagnosis, treatment, and bills
- Medical necessity of hospitalization
- Compliance with policy terms and limits

--------------------------------------------------
7. CLAIM APPROVAL CONDITIONS
--------------------------------------------------

A claim will be APPROVED if:

- Disease is covered under policy
- Total expenses exceed deductible (₹5,000)
- Documents are complete and valid
- Costs are within defined limits
- No exclusion criteria applies

--------------------------------------------------
8. CLAIM REJECTION CONDITIONS
--------------------------------------------------

A claim will be REJECTED if:

- Treatment is for excluded condition
- Documents are missing or inconsistent
- Expenses exceed policy limits without justification
- Pre-existing disease waiting period not met
- Non-medical or unnecessary procedures are billed

--------------------------------------------------
9. PARTIAL APPROVAL RULES
--------------------------------------------------

Partial claim may be approved when:

- Some expenses exceed allowed limits
- Certain items are not covered under policy
- Room rent exceeds allowed cap

--------------------------------------------------
10. FRAUD DETECTION RULES
--------------------------------------------------

Claim may be rejected if:

- Bills appear manipulated
- Duplicate claims are detected
- Mismatch in patient data across documents
- Fake prescriptions or lab reports identified

--------------------------------------------------
11. SPECIAL NOTES
--------------------------------------------------

- Diabetes-related treatments are covered if diagnosed after policy start OR waiting period satisfied
- Routine monitoring tests are covered if linked to a valid diagnosis
- Emergency hospitalization is always prioritized

--------------------------------------------------
FINAL DECISION LOGIC
--------------------------------------------------

APPROVED:
✔ Covered disease
✔ Valid documents
✔ Costs within limits

REJECTED:
✘ Exclusion triggered
✘ Missing documents
✘ Policy violation

PARTIALLY APPROVED:
✔ Some valid expenses
✘ Some non-covered costs
